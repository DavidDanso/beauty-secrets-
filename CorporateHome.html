THE GENESIS OF OUR BRAND

Beauty Secrets grew from the passion of the founder and CEO, Francesca Brenda Opoku. She grew up in Northern Ghana where her mother ran a vocational school. Growing up in a region that specialises in shea butter, she learnt at a young age how to make shea butter and other African beauty skincare products. In her quest to share what she learnt; Beauty Secrets was birthed. This venture has been a success as she has been able to create quality Ghanaian skincare products and make a tangible social impact.
