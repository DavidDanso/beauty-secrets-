FAQ's

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer for payments of Bulkpurchases and wholesale of Beauty Secrets products.


My transaction has failed, what do I do?

Please contact your card issuer directly to resolve any problem that has occurred with your credit card transaction. We do not authorise your card, your card issuer does. We cannot see your private details or the reason why your purchase has been declined.


Can I order a product via email?

Sorry, no. All Beauty Secrets sales need to be made via our website.


Do you offer discounts when I purchase more than one product?

We offer discount prices for bulk purchases only.Kindly refer to the conditions of a bulk purchase.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Regardless of your location, we deliver at your doorstep. We deliver on the same day as requested unless there is an issue with our delivery service, which will be communicated to you.

I bought a product from your site and I've decided that I don't really want it. Can I send it back to you for a refund?

Sorry, no. We do not allow returns or issue refunds for change of mind, so Please select your Beauty Secrets products carefully and read products details carefully before making purchase.

If this has happened to you then we sincerely apologise for this and for any inconvenience caused. Please contact us at sales@beautysecretsafrica.com to arrange an exchange for the same item.

Are your products FDA approved?

From acceptance of raw materials to delivery, we guarantee a smooth cosmetic manufacture. Every stage of production is under safe, quality standards and FDA approved procedures that is guaranteed to give results.


Do your products expire?

The average shelf life for our products is 2 years but for optimal freshness, we recommend using within the first 6-12 months of purchase.


Where are your products made?

Our products are crafted and batched in Ghana.


How should I store your products?

We recommend storing our products in a cool, dry, dark place to protect the cold-pressed oils and active ingredients.

Every skin is different and unique what works for someone within a particular time frame may not work for another. For some of our clients results are instant while others require consistency. In all we assure you that our products are effective.
